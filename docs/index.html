King for Kong

Manage Kong with ARCHITECT

Create routes, services, and more in a visual way!

ARCHITECT provides a network display mode to help you manage your Kong nodes. With just one look you will be able to analyze your node's elements relations, such as what upstreams are assigned to one route or the plugins enabled on a service.

All the information at hand

Full English interface, y también en Español!

King displays the most important information organized in categories, providing the option of downloading the raw JSON.

Don't miss anything: node configuration, database, plugins, services, routes, upstreams, consumers, certificates... Catch'em all!
